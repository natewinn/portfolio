Web Design

Photoshop + Illustrator

While Director of North Valley Publications, the website became our customers' primary method of ordering. However, the site aged, it became increasingly apparent that a face lift was needed, not just for a new look, but to add greater functionality. And to provide our customers with a pleasant experience and ensure customers were getting the very best service possible, I completely redesigned the website from the ground up.

Responsive First

Customers, more than ever, were navigating the site with a smart phone or tablet. This was apparent with site analytics and MailChimp campaign analytics. So, responsive design was a must. For the mobile view, I excluded anything that wasn't absolutely essential to easily and efficiently place an order on a mobile device.

Express Checkout

The checkout displayed important information including coupon codes or related items that a customer may have missed while shopping. In addition, we found customers were often navigating away from the order page to find help. Live chat and the customer service number was also placed within reach. In addition, customers would now have the ability to easily update their shopping cart, unlike the previous site.

From the Ground Up

Everything was reconsidered when the layout and navigation was designed. I wanted to make the product paramount—no social media icons or sharing ability was included to possibly distract the customer. Only the most essential information would be available to ensure an enjoyable shopping experience.
