About Me

At the age of thirty, I set out to pursue a dream - to make an even greater impact with the work I do. And to do this, I knew I must learn the language of the future. I was so serious about it that I left my job as a director at my church, North Valley Baptist Church, to complete an immersive three-month Ruby on Rails coding bootcamp at The Iron Yard. Now, I'm ready to roll up my sleeves and contribute my love for design, project management, and coding to an incredible team.

I live in the heart of the Silicon Valley and enjoy exploring Northern California with my wife Jo and my little boy, Owen. Away from work, you'll find me aeropressing coffee, running, reading on my kindle, or writing at natewinn.com.
