Email Designs

Graphic + Web Design

I quickly learned while at North Valley Publications that direct email marketing provided far greater ROI than any social media campaign. MailChimp was used to maintain mailing lists, track campaign analytics, and setup A/B testing. Below, you can see just a few examples of the customer emails that I created, designed, and coded for special events, product launches, and product sales.

http://nvpublications.org/first_friday_email

First Come Friday

A catchy email sent with a unique link available only to customers on the mailing list. Typically, the featured item would be a low inventory item that was marked down to eliminate the stock. However, customers would inevitably add additional products while purchasing the featured item. During one particular First Friday Sale, the sale item was only $8, but the average customer transaction was $135.61!

http://nvpublications.org/sunday_school_resources

Sunday School

The Sunday school curriculum was an quarterly item that needed to be reordered every three months to maintain the teaching schedule. This email acted both as a reminder to customers currently using the curriculum and as an advertisement to customers that were unaware this particular line of products was available for purchase.

http://nvpublications.org/march_madness

March Madness

Emails landing in customers' inboxes would often correspond with holidays or current events. March Merchandise Madness was a fun way to eliminate the inventory of many scratch and dent items during the NCAA March Madness Tournament.

http://nvpublications.org/music_resources

Music Resources

An email would always accompany the launch of a new product. Typically, a coupon code or special bundle offer would be included in the email as a benefit for customers belonging to the mailing list. In addition, launching a new product was used as an opportunity to cross promote related products.

http://nvpublications.org/black_friday_sale

Black Friday Sale

Just like many other retailers, Black Friday was the biggest sales day of the year. This was due in large part to the email sent to our customers building anticipation for the big sale. Though the sale was also promoted with social media during Black Friday, direct email saw the largest click-through of any campaign.

http://nvpublications.org/tax_day_sale

Tax Day Sale

On special days throughout the year, a unique coupon code was created that related to the special day. Tax Day was 10.40% off, Fourth of July was 17.76% off, and Columbus Day was 14.92% off. This was just another creative way to keep our product offering before the most loyal customers.
