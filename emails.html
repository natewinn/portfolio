Email Designs

Graphic + Web Design

I quickly learned while at North Valley Publications that direct email marketing provided far greater sales results than any social media campaign. MailChimp was used to maintain customer mailing lists, track campaign analytics, and automate email. Below, you can see a few examples of customer emails that I created, designed, and coded for special events, product launches, or sales.

http://nvpublications.org/first_friday_email

Frist Come Friday

A catchy email sent with a unique link only available to those on the mailing list. Typically, this would be a low inventory item that was marked down to eliminate the stock. However, customers would almost always order additional products. One particular sale, the item sale item was only $8, but the average customer transaction was $135.61.

http://nvpublications.org/sunday_school_resources

Sunday School

The Sunday school curriculum was an item that needed to be reordered every three months. This email acted both as a reminder to customers currently using the curriculum and as an advertisement to customers that were unaware that this line of products was available.

http://nvpublications.org/march_madness

March Madness

We often sent emails that would correspond with holidays or current events. March Merchandise Madness was a fun way to eliminate the inventory of many scratch and dent items that were typically written off during the NCAA March Madness Tournament.

http://nvpublications.org/music_resources

Music Resources

We would always send out an email to announce the launch of a new product. Typically, we would also offer a coupon code included in the email as a benefit for being on our mailing list. In addition, we took the opportunity to cross promote existing related products.

http://nvpublications.org/black_friday_sale

Black Friday Sale

Just like many other retailers, Black Friday was the biggest sales day of the year. This was due in large part to the direct email sent to our mail lists, though we did much with social media during Black Friday. Direct email saw the largest click-through of any campaign we created.

http://nvpublications.org/tax_day_sale

Tax Day Sale

On special days throughout the year, we would create a special coupon code that would relate to the special day. Tax Day was 10.40% off, Fourth of July was 17.76% off, and Columbus Day was 14.92% off. This was just another creative way to keep our product offering before our customers.
